One of the older BadPopcorn posts has started getting comment spam, so I decided to check out what spam protection I could install with WordPress (software that runs the site). I installed the Akismet plugin. It's already caught a few more spams, but let one pass. It's better than nothing, but it will take time to see if it really works.

A side note: I had to create another username/password pair to use Akismet, aarrg.
