build what you believe in

At BadPopcorn, we only work on projects we believe in. We feel that being passionate about a project is key in building great websites. Over the past three years we have become passionate about CrossFit. We built beyond the whiteboard to help CrossFit Affiliates organize, analyze and grow their CrossFit gym.

This idea is not our business strategy. It's our philosophy. We are going to make products that offer benefits. Products that make life simpler and more efficient. Sadly, this makes us unique.
